Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Snazzy Paws Grooming collects, uses and protects your personal information. Our goal is to keep your details safe while providing a friendly and professional grooming service.

Who we are

Snazzy Paws Grooming is a small dog grooming business based in Hamilton, New Zealand. We operate a local salon where customers can book grooming appointments for their dogs.

What information we collect

We collect only the information we need to run our grooming appointments and respond to enquiries, such as:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number and email address).
  • Your dog’s name, breed and basic grooming needs.
  • Preferred appointment dates and times.
  • Any notes you share about your dog’s health, behaviour or coat condition.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Confirm, reschedule or cancel grooming appointments.
  • Contact you with questions about your booking or your dog’s needs.
  • Respond to general enquiries submitted through the contact form.

We do not sell your personal information or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Storage and security

Booking details and contact messages may be stored in our email inbox and simple booking records. Access to these records is limited to staff who need the information to manage appointments. We use password-protected accounts and keep devices up to date to help protect your data.

Information is kept only as long as it is reasonably needed for business, appointment history or tax purposes, after which it is deleted or securely removed.

Cookies and analytics

At present, the Snazzy Paws website is a simple brochure site and booking form. If we add analytics tools in the future (such as Google Analytics), they will be used only to collect anonymous information about how people use the site, such as which pages are visited and which devices are used.

Any analytics cookies would not store names or contact details, and we will update this policy if the way we use analytics changes.

Your rights

You can contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections if your details are out of date or inaccurate.
  • Ask us to delete information where it is no longer required.

Contacting us about privacy

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please get in touch using the details on our Contact page.
